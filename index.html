I've had the fortune of participating in a range of interesting research projects with talented and patient collaborators in my academic career.

Convenience Store Item Demand Forecasting

2019.7- University of Tsukuba & FamilyMart Taiwan

This is an International Industry-University Collaboration research of University of Tsukuba and FamilyMart Taiwan. FamilyMart Taiwan managers are tasked with predicting their daily sales for at least one day in advance, our lab are appointed to develop new AI algorithms to make time series prediction and deploy them to Microsoft Cloud Service Azure as the software platform.

Image Retrieval Using Feature Extraction with CNNs

2018.1-2018.6 Donghua University

How to quickly and accurately obtain valuable image information is essential for data management. I assume and accomplish an idea about using CNNs which used transfer learning to make Alexnet (2012 Imagenet champion) can classify and retrieve similar images with input images.

Web Fashion Apparel Sensing and Recommendation

2017.1-2018.6 Donghua University

Previously, I participated in design and development of costuming recommendation on E-commerce platform. My responsibility is to design image retrieval systems which used Convolutional Neural Networks (CNNs). This is also a part of my graduation thesis which mentored by professor Mingbo Zhao.

Sonic Fire Extinguisher

2016.1-2016.8 Donghua University

This is a university business project relates to a low-frequency acoustic wave fire extinguisher, which can use acoustic energy to extinguish fires in various special occasions without causing other adverse effects on surrounding objects. In this project, I had experience on design and build the power signal generator which can product different signal waves so that extinguisher can output different sound amplitude and frequency.
